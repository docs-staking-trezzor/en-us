What is Staking?

Staking is the process of locking up your crypto assets in a blockchain network to support its operations and validate transactions. In return, stakers earn rewards in the form of additional cryptocurrency. Staking is available with several proof-of-stake (PoS) networks, offering an efficient and eco-friendly alternative to traditional mining.

Why Stake with Trezor?

  1. Enhanced Security: Unlike online wallets or exchanges, Trezor is a hardware wallet, keeping your private keys offline. This secure environment means that even while your assets are staked, they are less vulnerable to cyber threats.

  2. Control Over Assets: With Trezor staking, you maintain full control over your crypto. You decide how much to stake and when to withdraw, giving you flexibility alongside the security.

  3. Trezor Suite Integration: The latest Trezor Suite update (Version 24.9.2) includes staking support, making it easier than ever to manage staking directly through the Trezor interface.
